My favorite video game is Elden Ring.

Description:
Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game was made in collaboration with fantasy novelist George R. R. Martin, who provided material for the game's setting.
Release Date:
25th February, 2022
Different attributes and what they mean:
Vigor: Attribute governing HP(Hit Points) or the health of the character.
Mind: Attribute governing FP(Focus Points) or the mana points of the character.
Endurance: Attribute governing stamina of the character.
Strength: Attribute governing the overall strength of the character.
Dexterity: Attribute required to wield advanced armaments/dual wielding
Intelligence: Attribute required to perform magic attacks; also governs magic damage.
Faith: Attribute required to perform incantatoins.
Arcane: Attribute governing item discovery in the open world.
